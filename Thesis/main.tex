\section*{Abstract}
A modified Multilevel Network Meta-Regression (ML-NMR) was performed on data from eight studies comparing Gemcitabine (GEM) with six combination therapies and one standalone therapy. The comparative efficacy of the combination therapies was assessed in terms of the median overall survival (OS) and restricted mean survival time (RMST) estimates. GEM in combination with nab-paclitaxel (NAB) or capecitabine (CAP) provides improved median OS and RMST estimates for the treatment of advanced/metastatic pancreatic cancer compared to GEM monotherapy.
