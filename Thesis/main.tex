\section*{Abstract}
Five treatments for advanced pancreatic cancer were compared against the standard of care treatment in two network meta analyses. The first network meta analysis used parametric survival models, and the second used fractional polynomials. The parametric model results showed only one treatment gave improvements on the standard of care, but little to no improvements were found in overall survival.
